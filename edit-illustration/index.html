ChatBots Gone Awry

Twitter Bot Tay

Microsoft

March 24, 2016

Microsoft launched its new Twitter bot, Tay, encouraging users to engage it in conversation. Within 24 hours, the bot began spewing vitriol such as “I fucking hate feminists and they should all burn in hell.”

Tay exists as testament to the question if we use public data to train an AI, which public data should be used? If we want an AI to reflect the capabilities of the human mind and body, but if most people hold unconscious biases, how do we eliminate that?
